    You can interact with the 3D scene in several ways:
  • Use your computer's mouse to orbit, pan, and zoom around the scene. You can also use keyboard controls: hold W/A/S/D or arrow keys to move smoothly, Q/E to move up/down, and R to reset the camera to default view.
  • Customise your brick before adding it to the scene. Choose a colour and adjust its size using the width, height, and depth inputs.
  • Click on the Add Brick button to place a new brick in the scene.
  • Click on the Add Random Brick button to place a new randomised brick in the scene.
  • Click on the Reset Camera button to return the camera to its default view.

Your browser does not support WebGL. Please use a compatible browser.
